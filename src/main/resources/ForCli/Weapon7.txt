0.basic mode: Move a target 0, 1, or 2 squares to a square you can see, and give it 1 damage.
1.@|bold,red █|@ @|bold,yellow █|@ in punisher mode: Choose a target 0, 1, or 2 moves away from you. Move the target to your square and deal 3 damage to it.
