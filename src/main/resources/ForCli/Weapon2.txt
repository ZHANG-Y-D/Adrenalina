@|bold 2.GRENADE LAUNCHER|@ [@|bold,red █|@]
0.basic effect: Deal 1 damage to 1 target you can see. Then you may move the target 1 square.
1.@|bold,red █|@ with extra grenade: Deal 1 damage to every player on a square you can see. You can use this before or after the basic effect's move.
Notes: For example, you can shoot a target, move it onto a square with other targets, then damage everyone including the first target.
       Or you can deal 2 to a main target, 1 to everyone else on that square, then move the main target. Or you can deal 1 to an isolated target and 1 to everyone on a different square. If you target your own square, you will not be moved or damaged.
