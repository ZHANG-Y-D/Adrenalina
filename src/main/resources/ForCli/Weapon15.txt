0.basic mode: Choose 1 target on any square exactly 1 move away. Move onto that square and give the target 1 damage and 2 marks.
1.@|bold,blue █ |@in rocket fist mode: Choose a square exactly 1 move away. Move onto that square. You may deal 2 damage to 1 target there.
    If you want, you may move 1 more square in that same direction (but only if it is a legal move). You may deal 2 damage to 1 target there, as well.
Notes: In rocket fist mode, you're flying 2 squares in a straight line, punching 1 person per square.
